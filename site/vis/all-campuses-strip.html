CU System — Salary by Job Family

COL-adjusted median salary · FY 2025–26 · All campuses pooled · 100% FTE only · One dot per employee

Each dot represents one full-time (100% FTE) employee across the entire CU system. Salaries are COL-adjusted per campus before pooling — employees at higher cost-of-living locations are deflated to a common baseline before aggregation. Job families are sorted by median COL-adjusted salary, highest first. Box shows IQR; whiskers extend to 1.5×IQR fence; center line is median. Data source: CU Salary Database.
