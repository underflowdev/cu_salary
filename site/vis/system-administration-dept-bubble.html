COL-adjusted median salary · FY 2025–26 · 100% FTE only · Bubble area ∝ headcount · departments with ≥ 10 FTE

Each bubble represents one department in System Administration. Vertical position indicates median COL-adjusted annual salary; bubble area is proportional to headcount. Departments with fewer than 10 full-time employees are excluded. Bubbles are positioned using a static force layout that preserves the salary axis while resolving overlaps. Hover a bubble for department name and quartiles. Data source: CU Salary Database.
