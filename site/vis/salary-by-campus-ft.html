CU Employee Salaries by Campus — Full-Time Only

COL-adjusted annual base salary · FY 2025–26 · One dot per employee · 100% FTE appointments only

Each point represents one full-time (100% FTE) employment record in the CU system for FY 2025–26. Part-time, fractional, and records with missing FTE values are excluded. Salary is annual base pay; additional allowances, bonuses, or contractual additions are not included. Employees with multiple appointments may appear more than once if each appointment is full-time. Data source: CU Salary Database.
