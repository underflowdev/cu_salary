CU Boulder — Regular Faculty Salary by Department

COL-adjusted annual base salary · FY 2025–26 · 100% FTE · Regular Faculty only · departments with ≥ 5 FTE

Each point is one Regular Faculty employment record at CU Boulder, positioned by annual base salary adjusted for Boulder's cost of living index (×1.41 relative to national baseline). Regular Faculty includes tenured, tenure-track, and other permanent faculty lines. Departments are sorted by median salary, highest first. Departments with fewer than 5 qualifying full-time employees are excluded. Box shows IQR; whiskers extend to 1.5×IQR fence; center line is median. Points beyond the whisker fence are suppressed and counted as +n above each column. Scroll horizontally to see all departments. Data source: CU Salary Database.
