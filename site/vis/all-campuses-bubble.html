CU System — Job Family Bubbles

COL-adjusted median salary · FY 2025–26 · All campuses pooled · 100% FTE only · Bubble area ∝ headcount

Each bubble represents one job family across the entire CU system. Salaries are COL-adjusted per campus before pooling — employees at higher cost-of-living locations are deflated to a common baseline before aggregation. Vertical position indicates median COL-adjusted annual salary; bubble area is proportional to total system-wide headcount in that job family. Data source: CU Salary Database.
