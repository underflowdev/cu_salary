CU System — Job Families by Campus

COL-adjusted median salary · FY 2025–26 · 100% FTE only · One bubble per campus × job family · Bubble area ∝ headcount

Each bubble represents one job family at one campus. Campuses are arranged in columns sharing a common Y axis (median COL-adjusted annual salary), allowing direct salary comparison across campuses for the same job family. Bubble area is proportional to headcount within that campus. A force layout resolves overlaps while preserving vertical salary position. Data source: CU Salary Database.
